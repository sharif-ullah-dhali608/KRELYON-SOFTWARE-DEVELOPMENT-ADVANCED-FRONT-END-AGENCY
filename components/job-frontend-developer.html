Senior Frontend Developer

Remote (Global) / Full-Time

Apply Now

We are looking for a passionate Senior Frontend Developer to join our growing team. You will be responsible for building beautiful, high-performing user interfaces for our clients' digital products. You'll work closely with our UI/UX designers and backend engineers to bring ideas to life.

Responsibilities

  • Develop new user-facing features using React.js and Next.js.
  • Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality, responsive code.
  • Optimize components for maximum performance across a vast array of web-capable devices and browsers.
  • Mentor junior developers and participate in code reviews.
  • Collaborate with designers to ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of professional experience in frontend development.
  • Expert proficiency in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3.
  • Strong experience with React.js and its ecosystem (Redux, Context API).
  • Experience with server-side rendering (Next.js) is a huge plus.
  • Familiarity with code versioning tools, such as Git.
  • A keen eye for design and user experience.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits.
  • A fully remote and flexible work environment.
  • Opportunities to work on diverse and challenging projects.
  • A collaborative team culture that values learning and growth.

A creative partner for digital innovators. We solve complex problems with human-centric design.

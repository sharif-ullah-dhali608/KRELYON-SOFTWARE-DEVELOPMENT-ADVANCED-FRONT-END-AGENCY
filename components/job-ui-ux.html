UI/UX Designer

Dhaka, Bangladesh / Full-Time

Apply Now

We're looking for a talented UI/UX Designer to create intuitive, accessible, and beautiful user experiences. You will be the bridge between user needs, business goals, and our engineering team, transforming complex ideas into elegant solutions.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct user research, interviews, and usability testing to gather insights.
  • Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, and site maps to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas.
  • Develop high-fidelity mockups, prototypes, and design specifications.
  • Collaborate closely with developers to ensure design-to-code accuracy and implementation.
  • Help maintain and evolve our design system to ensure consistency across products.

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of professional experience in UI/UX design for web and mobile apps.
  • A strong portfolio showcasing your design process and high-quality work.
  • Expert proficiency in Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.
  • Solid understanding of user-centered design principles and methodologies.
  • Excellent visual design skills and a good sense of user-empathy.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits.
  • A creative and collaborative work environment.
  • Opportunities to work on diverse and challenging projects.
  • A team culture that values learning, growth, and innovation.

A creative partner for digital innovators. We solve complex problems with human-centric design.
